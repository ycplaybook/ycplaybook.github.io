The Yogi Cup Playbook is a manual to planning Yogi Cup. It also serves as a way to reference specific files from a previous Yogi Cup.

All documents provided on this site link to NE Yogi Cup 2020 planning files.

Playbook Subdivisions:

1. Timeline - provides a general list of tasks that must be completed before the event in chronological order (similar to a Backwards Plan)

2. Departments - provides a detailed breakdown of the tasks and responsibilities of each department

3. Guides - provides instructions on the completion of some of the more complex tasks in Yogi Cup planning

When planning Yogi Cup, keep in mind that the Playbook is just a generic guide - adding new, creative, and fun features to every Yogi Cup is still expected of the team.
