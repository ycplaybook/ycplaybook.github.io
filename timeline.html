Yogi Cup Timeline

Yogi Cup 2020 Folder

These milestones are completed by RCTLs, RCs, and other SDC karyakars. Since it is difficult to set (and enforce) hard deadlines more than 4 months before the event, most of these milestones are not included in the timeline below. Nevertheless, it is imperative that these milestones are completed by/before the suggested deadline: a delay in the completion of these milestones will slow down the rest of Yogi Cup planning.
  • T-12 months: Budget Approval
    • Yogi Cup Budget should be approved by Leads + Lead Sant at Annual Budget Meeting
  • T-9 months: Book Venue
    • If looking for a new venue, the venue search should be commenced several months before this deadline
    • Assume that there will be a 15-20% increase in the number of TEAMS participating in this year's Yogi Cup than from the previous year
    • The venue should have:
      1. Enough courts to accommodate the estimated number of teams
      2. Enough space for kishores to eat (if planning to spend the whole day at facility)
      3. Sufficient storage area
      4. Sufficient number of strategy rooms
  • T-6 months: Research Hotels + Book Rooms
    • Assume that there will be a 15-20% increase in the number of PLAYERS + VOLUNTEERS participating in this year's Yogi Cup than from the previous year
    • If Yogi Cup will be played in the same venue/area it was played the previous year:
      • Reach out to the hotels where players + volunteers stayed the previous year (unless we find better hotels in the area). Keeping the estimated number of players + volunteers for this year's Yogi Cup in mind, book the appropriate number of rooms.
    • If Yogi Cup will be NOT played in the same venue/area it was played the previous year:
      • Start reaching out to hotels in the area weeks/months before this deadline
      • Once suitable hotels have been found, book the appropriate number of rooms while keeping the estimated number of players + volunteers in mind
  • T-4 months: Finalize Registration Plan
    • RAs and RCTLs should work together to finalize Registration Plan
    • Registration Plan must be finalized + approved at the Regional Leads Meeting 4 months before Yogi Cup
    • A sample registration plan can be found in the "Registration" department breakdown
